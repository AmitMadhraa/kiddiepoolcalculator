Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I change the water in my kiddie pool?

For small pools (under 300 gallons):
- Without chemicals: Every 1-3 days
- With proper treatment: Every 1-2 weeks

For larger pools (300+ gallons):
- Without chemicals: Every 3-5 days
- With proper treatment: Every 2-4 weeks

Change water more frequently if you notice debris, cloudiness, algae, or odors.

What's the minimum maintenance required?

At minimum, you should:
- Skim debris daily
- Cover the pool when not in use
- Change water regularly
- Clean surfaces during water changes
- Store properly when not in use

For very small pools used by young children, simply empty, rinse, and refill every 1-2 days.

How do I keep my kiddie pool clean without chemicals?

To maintain without chemicals:
- Change water every 1-3 days
- Use a pool cover
- Have swimmers rinse off before entering
- Skim debris daily
- Clean with vinegar solution
- Place away from debris sources
- Consider a small filter
- Dry completely when not in use

What's the best maintenance schedule for an inflatable pool?

For inflatable pools:
- Daily: Skim debris, cover when not in use
- Every 1-3 days/weekly: Complete water change
- During water changes: Wipe surfaces, check for leaks
- Weekly: Check air pressure, inspect valves
- Monthly: Deep clean with vinegar solution
- Seasonally: Clean thoroughly before storage
