How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select pool shape - Choose circular, rectangular, or oval based on your pool's shape.
  2. Enter dimensions - Provide measurements in your preferred units:
    • Circular: diameter (widest part)
    • Rectangular: length and width
    • Oval: longest and widest points
  3. Enter water depth - How deep you plan to fill the pool (typically less than full wall height).
  4. Calculate - Get instant results for volume, fill time, and water cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I measure my kiddie pool for water volume?

Measure the interior dimensions at water level. For circular pools: diameter and depth. For rectangular: length, width, and depth. For oval: longest and widest points, plus depth. Use consistent units (inches, feet, or cm).

How much water does a typical kiddie pool hold?

Small pool (4ft diameter, 10in deep): ~65 gallons (246L)
Medium pool (6ft diameter, 15in deep): ~220 gallons (833L)

How long does it take to fill a kiddie pool?

With a standard garden hose (13 GPM average):
Small pool (65 gallons): 5 minutes
Medium pool (220 gallons): 17 minutes
Large pool (465 gallons): 36 minutes

Times vary based on water pressure and hose diameter.

How much does it cost to fill a kiddie pool?

At average US rates ($0.004/gallon):
Small pool (65 gallons): ~$0.26
Medium pool (220 gallons): ~$0.88
Large pool (465 gallons): ~$1.86

Check local water rates for precise costs.

How often should I change the water?

Small pools without filtration: Every 1-2 days
Larger pools with chemical treatment: Every 1-2 weeks

Factors include usage, debris, sunlight exposure, and chemical treatment. Try our Water Replacement Schedule Calculator for personalized recommendations.
